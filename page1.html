About Me

My name is Erica, I'm 22 years old, and I'm a personal stylist based out of Toronto, Canada. I discovered from a very young age that I had a deep affinity for fashion. I've designed in school fashion shows since childhood which is where my passion for fashion design and creative styling grew. During my four years of undergrad at Western University,. was the head fashion stylist for Fashion and Lifestyle Society (FLS) during all 4 years of my undergrad at Western University.

Here I led a team of likeminded individuals, mentored younger/newer stylists throughout the years, and styled and creative directed various photoshoots.
I had the opportunity to style all looks for both fashion shows this past year, and have helped to style all fashion shows during my time at western (2019-2023) - 
Overlooked everything - 
Brought directors’ visions to life through styling according to THEIR preferences and vision and applying my skills and experience to achieve that
